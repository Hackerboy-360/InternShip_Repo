Who We Are

The PulseForge Story

PulseForge was born in a Bangalore garage in 2020. Three fitness-obsessed engineers — fed up paying premium gym membership fees and getting nothing but mediocre equipment in return — decided to build something better.

Their mission: engineer smart fitness gear that merges intelligent tracking, clinical-grade materials, and thoughtful industrial design. The kind of equipment that used to exist only in elite performance centres — now available for every home gym.

Today PulseForge ships nationwide across India. Over 50,000 athletes rely on our gear daily — from first-time home gym builders to national-level competitive CrossFitters. Every single product is designed, tested and warrantied in-house.
